Features

Variable Font Precision

Unusual weight stops at 320, 330, 340, 450, 480, 540. Hierarchy through micro-differences, not blunt instruments.

Pill & Circle Geometry

50px radius pills for buttons, 50% circles for icon actions. Dashed focus outlines echo editor selection handles.

Monochrome Chrome

Pure black #000000 and white #ffffff only. Color exists exclusively in product content and hero gradients.
