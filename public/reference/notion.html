Notion Design System

Build beautiful products faster

A blank canvas that gets out of your way. Warm minimalism that feels like quality paper, not sterile glass.

Features

Warm Neutrals

Every gray carries yellow-brown undertones. #f6f5f4 warm white, #615d59 body text, #a39e98 muted labels.

Whisper Borders

Ultra-thin 1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.1) everywhere. Structure without weight, like pencil lines on paper.

Layered Shadows

4-5 layer shadow stacks with max 0.05 opacity per layer. Depth that is felt rather than seen.
