Linear Design System

Build beautiful products faster

Dark-mode-native product design where content emerges from darkness like starlight. Precision-engineered hierarchy through luminance.

Features

Luminance Stacking

Elevation via background opacity stepping: rgba(255,255,255, 0.02 to 0.05). Never solid backgrounds on dark.

Weight 510 Signature

The signature between-weight that sits between regular and medium. Subtle emphasis without the heaviness of semibold.

Transparent Borders

Semi-transparent white borders — rgba(255,255,255,0.05) to 0.08 — creating structure without visual noise.
